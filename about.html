Our Mission

To democratize access to secure voting technology, enabling organizations of all sizes to conduct fair, transparent, and trustworthy elections. We believe every vote should count and every voice should be heard.

Our Vision

A world where digital elections are the trusted standard - secure, accessible, and verifiable. We envision a future where geographic barriers and logistical challenges no longer prevent democratic participation.

Our Story

elects.online was founded with a simple belief: secure, transparent elections shouldn't be a privilege reserved for those with deep pockets or technical expertise. We saw organizations struggling with outdated voting methods - paper ballots prone to errors, expensive third-party services, and systems that couldn't scale.

Our team of security experts, software engineers, and democracy advocates came together to build something different. Today, elects.online powers elections for universities, corporations, nonprofits, and community organizations worldwide - all with the same commitment to security, transparency, and accessibility that drove us from day one.
