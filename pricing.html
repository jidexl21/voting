Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

What's the difference between Individual and Agent plans?

Individual plans are one-time payments per election, perfect for occasional use. Agent plans are subscriptions for consultants and agencies who run multiple elections regularly.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
