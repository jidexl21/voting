Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

elects.online ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online voting platform and services. By using elects.online, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • ✓ Name, email address, and contact information
  • ✓ Account credentials (encrypted passwords)
  • ✓ Profile photos (if provided for voter verification)
  • ✓ Organization and election-related information

Usage Information

  • ✓ Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • ✓ IP address and general location data
  • ✓ Pages visited and actions taken on our platform
  • ✓ Voting activity timestamps (not vote content)

How We Use Your Information

Provide Services

Operate and maintain our voting platform, process votes, and deliver results.

Ensure Security

Verify voter identity, prevent fraud, and maintain election integrity.

Communications

Send election notifications, updates, and account information.

Improve Platform

Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and experience.

Your Vote is Private

We take vote privacy extremely seriously. Your actual vote choices are encrypted and cannot be viewed by elects.online staff, election administrators, or any third party.

  • ✓ Votes are encrypted end-to-end using industry-standard cryptography
  • ✓ Vote content is separated from voter identity in our systems
  • ✓ Election results are aggregated without exposing individual votes
  • ✓ Audit logs track participation, not vote choices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • 1 Access your personal information that we hold
  • 2 Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • 3 Delete your account and associated data
  • 4 Export your data in a portable format
  • 5 Opt-out of marketing communications

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers

Secure Servers

Data stored in secure, monitored data centers

Access Controls

Strict access controls and authentication

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Election data is retained according to the requirements set by election administrators and applicable laws. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
