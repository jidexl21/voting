Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first election?

After signing up, click "Create Election" from your dashboard. Follow the step-by-step wizard to set up your ballot, add candidates, configure voter access, and schedule your election.

How do voters access the election?

Voters receive a unique link via email. They can also access elections through a voter portal using their registered email address.

Can I customize the look of my ballot?

Yes! You can add your organization's logo, customize colors, and include candidate photos and bios to create a branded voting experience.

How secure is the voting process?

All votes are encrypted end-to-end. We use bank-grade security measures including SSL encryption, secure data centers, and regular security audits.

Can voters change their vote?

This is configurable. You can allow voters to modify their vote until the election closes, or lock votes immediately after submission.

How do I export election results?

Results can be exported as PDF reports or CSV files. You can also generate detailed audit reports for compliance purposes.
